Our Story

A Family Business,
Built in Turlock

The Tire Boys was founded right here in Turlock with one goal in mind: Treat everyone like family. Charles and Bobby opened the first shop together in 2002, passing down what they knew to their kids and nephews — turning a two-man operation into a full family business.

Over the years, we moved through three locations across Turlock before finding our permanent home at 400 S Golden State Blvd. Through every move, one thing stayed constant: a commitment to honest work and fair prices for every customer who walks through the door.

We're not a chain. We're your neighbors. And that makes all the difference.
